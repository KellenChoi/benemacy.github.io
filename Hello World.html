Welcome to Benemacy

Kellen Choi, Benemacy Inc.

Founder & President

My cultural heritage taught me values of selflessness and compassion from a young age. Throughout my life, looking for unique opportunities to serve others is ingrained in my attitude and behavior. My association with charitable organizations for over five years no doubt gave me the chance to become increasingly aware of diverse social issues and mutual aid. In the Korean language, the word that comes closest to philanthropy is nanum (나눔) and translated literally means “sharing.” It is a larger concept than the traditional definition of voluntary action for the common good because it incorporates all types of sharing, from one’s food to one’s assistance to one’s wealth. So here I am to take social responsilities by promoting innovative and colloborative solutions to make our world better.
