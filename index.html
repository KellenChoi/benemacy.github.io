Kellen Choi, Benemacy Inc.

Founder & President

I am a government-endorsed unofficial ambassador and national from the Republic of Korea. My cultural heritage taught me values of selflessness and compassion from a young age. Throughout my life, looking for unique opportunities to serve others is ingrained in my life priniciples. My association with charitable organizations for the past years no doubt gave me the chance to become increasingly aware of diverse social issues and mutual aid. So here I am to take social responsilities by promoting innovative and colloborative solutions to make our world better.
